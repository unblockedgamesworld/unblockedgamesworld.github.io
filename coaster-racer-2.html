Coaster Racer 2

Description

Let's show off your racing skills in Coaster Racer 2. Maneuver your car or motorbike to complete all challenges in the game and gain as much money as possible.

This game is inviting fans of racing games such as Racing Horizon or Hill Climb Racing 2. In this game, you will get a chance to drive a car or motorbike to race on the highway. You must complete different missions to earn as much money as possible. For example, you have to race with the other racer or catch up with the designated driver. All missions are easy, so I believe you can accomplish them in the shortest time.

Play this game now and don't forget to check out the hottest game which is Slope Game on our website.

How to control

Press WASD or arrow keys to drive

Press Z or M to do freestyle

Press X or N to use nitro
