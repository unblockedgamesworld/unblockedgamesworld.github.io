Red Light Green Light

Description

Red Light Green Light is a 3D arcade game inspired by the Squid Game survival drama TV series. Push your luck and cross the finish line first.

The showrunners tortured the subjects on purpose by introducing new regulations and penalties. A single error, no matter how minor, might often result in death. And if a participant paused or attempted to leave, they were cruelly shot. The classic Red Light Green Light game requires rapid reflexes and reaction rates. It is critical to beat the competition to the goal. However, it is easy to get carried away in the excitement of the competition. Failure to stop on cue and taking an extra step disqualifies you. Which, according to the rules of the competition, means physical elimination. Fortunately, a virtual alternative has no negative consequences. Players have an unlimited number of attempts to succeed.

How to Play Red Light Green Light

  • The contenders are expected to sprint to the finish line.
  • They can only move when the permissive signal is active. When it changes to the restrictive state, everyone must shudder to a halt.
  • The smallest misstep or motion during the stillness period results in execution.

Controls:

Use mouse to move and stop.
